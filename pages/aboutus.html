BRIEF HISTORY OF KUREMU SECONDARY SCHOOL

Kuremu Secondary School was started way back in January 1998 . The school mushroomed from kuremu Primary School. The Primary school donated a dministration block plus some classes. In the year 1999, the Board of governors was appointed. It is regrettable that the school can presently only lay claim to about an acre following a land dispute with the neighbours which has dragged on for a longtime. The school has developed to its current state where it has an enrolment of 1020 students, 24 members of teaching staff, 13 members of support staff and several physical facilities. Kuremu Secondary School is a Mixed Public Day School. The school community still gears towards development to even greater heights.
