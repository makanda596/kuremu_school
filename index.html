EXCELLENCE IS OUR CULTURE

Kuremu School is a Kenyan county school located in butula Constituency, Busia County. This is within the western Province of Kenya. It was founded in 20001 with a student population currently at over 2000. The learning option for students is Boarding and day schoolers. Studies are carried out through a Kenyan based Curriculum.

We strive to be committed to the students we are privileged to serve, in our alumni association, our dedicated, talented department and staff. We also take pride in having the most active and helpful group of partners, visionary parents, well-wishers, alumni and friends.
